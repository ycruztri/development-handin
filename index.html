Summary

Amazon’s aggregator usage and recommendation features are good, as it provides a seamless shopping experience with easy access to the shopping cart and personalized recommendations. Pinterest stands out with its visually appealing board system and collaborative features, although improving access to favorited items could enhance usability. Etsy integrates favoriting seamlessly into product listings but could improve access and visibility of favorited items for a better user experience.

Implementation

  • Prominent Aggregator Usage + Easy Access
    • Ensure easy access to the list/aggregator → prominently displaying it
  • Visual Appeal and Organization
    • Create visually appealing and well-organized interface
  • Quick Actions
    • Include quick actions such as "Add to Favorites/Cart" buttons on product thumbnails

Functionality

Theme: Makeup Website

Intention of App: The intention of the app is to provide users with a convenient platform to explore and purchase makeup products. Users can browse through a diverse range of makeup items, filter them based on various categories such as product type, brand, and price range, sort them based on price or popularity, and add their preferred items to their shopping cart for purchase.

Features to Include:

  • Filtering Categories:
    • Brand
    • Product type
  • Sorting Feature:
    • Price
  • Aggregate Section:
    • Shopping cart
  • Reset Functionality:
    • Reset button
  • Item Card:
    • Item image (you can reuse a single image for all items)
    • Item name (this must be unique for each item)
    • Categories that your filter uses (e.g., price, ingredients)
    • Field(s) that your sort uses (e.g., price, name)
    • A button that adds to and removes from the aggregator, depending on if the item is already in the aggregator
